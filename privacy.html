JUHD Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to JUHD (“JUHD,” “we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how JUHD collects, uses, stores, processes, protects, and shares information when users access or use the JUHD mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).

JUHD processes personal data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, including the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), where applicable.

By using JUHD and submitting information through the Platform, users consent to the collection and processing of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials
  • Profile image
  • Date of birth or age range
  • City or region
  • Fitness goals and preferences

Fitness & Workout Information

JUHD may collect:

  • Workout logs
  • Exercises
  • Sets, reps, and weights
  • Workout duration
  • Body measurements
  • Nutrition or calorie-related information
  • Progress metrics
  • Saved routines

Community & Social Information

If users participate in community features, JUHD may collect:

  • Posts
  • Comments
  • Likes
  • Messages
  • Friend relationships
  • Trainer subscriptions
  • Gym check-ins
  • Uploaded photos or videos

Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Usage analytics
  • Diagnostic information
  • Crash logs

Location Information

If permission is granted, JUHD may collect approximate or precise location information to support:

  • City-based features
  • Gym check-ins
  • Nearby community features
  • Local rankings and leaderboards

Users may disable location permissions through device settings.

2. How We Use Information

JUHD may use information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Platform
  • Provide workout and social features
  • Personalize user experience
  • Improve Platform performance and functionality
  • Generate analytics and insights
  • Prevent spam, abuse, fraud, and unauthorized activity
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send service notifications and reminders
  • Enforce our policies and Community Guidelines
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

JUHD may also send promotional communications or updates related to the Platform. Users may opt out of certain non-essential communications where available.

3. AI & Recommendation Features

JUHD may use automated systems or AI-assisted features to generate summaries, recommendations, insights, or workout-related suggestions.

These features are informational only and should not be considered medical, nutritional, or professional advice.

4. Health & Fitness Disclaimer

Fitness and health-related information provided through JUHD is informational only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Users participate in workouts, fitness activities, and challenges voluntarily and at their own risk.

5. Social Visibility & User Content

Depending on Platform functionality and privacy settings, certain information may be visible to other users, including:

  • Username
  • Profile image
  • Workout summaries
  • Posts and comments
  • Likes and interactions
  • Gym check-ins
  • Trainer interactions

Users are responsible for the content they choose to share publicly or with others.

JUHD does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of user-generated content.

6. Third-Party Services

JUHD may integrate with third-party services including:

  • Apple Health
  • Spotify
  • Firebase
  • Supabase
  • Analytics providers
  • Payment providers

Use of third-party services may also be subject to those providers’ own terms and privacy policies.

JUHD does not sell personal user data to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

7. Data Storage & International Transfers

User information may be processed or stored on servers located outside the user’s country of residence, including outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

JUHD may transfer, process, or store data internationally using service providers that implement commercially reasonable safeguards and security measures.

By using JUHD, users acknowledge and consent to such transfers and processing.

8. Data Security

JUHD uses commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information.

However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure. Users acknowledge that they provide information at their own risk.

9. Security Incidents & Data Breaches

In the event of a security incident, unauthorized access, or data breach affecting user information, JUHD may investigate the matter, take corrective action, and notify affected users or competent authorities where required under applicable laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

10. Account Security Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.

Users should:

  • Use strong passwords
  • Protect login credentials
  • Avoid sharing account access
  • Notify JUHD immediately of suspicious or unauthorized activity

11. Disclosure Required by Law

JUHD may disclose information where required by:

  • Applicable laws
  • Court orders
  • Regulatory authorities
  • Governmental requests
  • Law enforcement obligations

12. Data Retention

JUHD retains information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Operate the Platform
  • Provide services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements

Deleted information may temporarily remain in backups, logs, or cached systems for a limited period.

13. User Rights & Choices

Subject to applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Modify account information
  • Manage privacy settings and permissions

Requests may be submitted through the Platform or by contacting support.

14. Children’s Privacy

JUHD is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Users under the age of majority in their jurisdiction should use the Platform only with appropriate parental or guardian permission.

15. Inactive Accounts

JUHD reserves the right to reclaim usernames, remove inactive accounts, or release unused usernames after extended periods of inactivity.

16. Changes to This Policy

JUHD may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the Platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
