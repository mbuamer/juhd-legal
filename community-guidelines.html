JUHD Community Guidelines

Last Updated: May 2026

JUHD exists to support effort, discipline, fitness, and community.

These Community Guidelines help maintain a respectful, safe, and motivating environment across the Platform.

All users are expected to follow these Guidelines.

1. Respect Other Users

Users may not:

  • Harass, bully, or threaten others
  • Shame users for body type, appearance, gender, fitness level, or goals
  • Spread hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Encourage targeted abuse or harassment

Constructive encouragement and healthy competition are welcome. Abuse is not.

2. Keep the Community Safe

Users may not:

  • Promote violence or criminal activity
  • Share dangerous fitness practices
  • Encourage self-harm or eating disorders
  • Sell illegal drugs, steroids, or prohibited substances
  • Share sexually explicit content

JUHD may remove dangerous content immediately.

3. Authenticity Matters

Users may not:

  • Impersonate others
  • Use fake identities
  • Mislead users about qualifications or achievements
  • Post deceptive or manipulated transformations intended to mislead

Trainer accounts should accurately represent certifications and experience.

4. Respect Privacy

Users may not:

  • Share private information without consent
  • Record or upload others in gyms without permission
  • Doxx users
  • Share confidential conversations or personal information

Respect both online and real-world privacy.

5. Spam & Promotion

Users may not:

  • Spam comments or messages
  • Use bots or unauthorized automation
  • Share malicious links
  • Scam users
  • Send repetitive unsolicited promotions

Business, trainer, or gym promotions must comply with Platform rules.

6. Intellectual Property

Only upload content you own or have permission to use.

Users may not upload copyrighted music, videos, photos, logos, or content without authorization.

7. Gym & Offline Conduct

Exercise caution when interacting with users offline.

JUHD is not responsible for offline interactions, gym meetings, or community encounters between users.

8. Reporting Violations

Users may report:

  • Harassment
  • Spam
  • Fake accounts
  • Dangerous content
  • Fraudulent behavior
  • Violations of these Guidelines

JUHD may review reports and take moderation action where appropriate.

9. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Feature restrictions
  • Temporary suspensions
  • Permanent account bans

Severe violations may be reported to competent authorities where legally required.

10. Our Goal

JUHD exists to build a positive fitness community focused on growth, effort, discipline, respect, and consistency.
